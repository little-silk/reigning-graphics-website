Cheese Chaser Repackage

Petlinks is a cat toy brand that provides eco-friendly products to enrich the lives of feline companions by "fulfill(ing) your pet's emotional, instinctual, and physical needs." Their goal is to offer consumers enviromentally friendly products with unique designs. The Cheese Chaser is an engaging remote controlled mouse that enriches your cat's life by activating their natural hunting instincts. This toy puts the fun of a mouse chase in your hands and encourages cat to exercise at the same time.​​​​​​ The product currently nullifies the company’s mission of eco-friendly packaging due to the excessive use of plastic.

I designed a fun package that is easily identifiable from other brands while promoting the brand’s mission of improving a cat’s lifestyle through eco-friendly toys. My process startd with brand research and sketches before I constructed three working prototypes. The three low-fidelity prototypes were made of paper and are held together by tape, allowing the user to hold a tangible prototype of potential packaging options. The final packaging will be a sliding box made of paperboard that represents a mouse trap. Design elements were pulled from the brand to decorate the interior and exterior of the box.
